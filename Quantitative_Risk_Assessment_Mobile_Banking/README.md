# Quantitative Cyber Risk Assessment: "SecureMobile" Banking Platform

**Date:** April 2026 | **Consultant:** Samuel Okunribido | **Status:** Final Deliverable

---

## 1. Executive Summary
This assessment quantifies the financial risk exposure of the SecureMobile platform across three critical attack vectors. Total Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE) is valued at **$35,000,000**. Strategic implementation of the recommended controls is projected to reduce exposure by **77%**, yielding a year-one ROI of **3,600%**.
